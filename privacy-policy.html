Introduction

Sub Tracker ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Subscription Data: Names, costs, and billing dates of subscriptions you enter. This data is stored locally on your device.

Data We Do NOT Collect

How We Use Your Information

Our app uses the following third-party services:

Data Storage and Security

Your subscription data is primarily stored locally on your device. If you enable cloud backup, your data is securely stored in Firebase with encryption in transit and at rest.

Your Rights
